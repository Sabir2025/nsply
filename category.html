Stern Tube Seal Systems & Chrome Steel Liners

We supply all necessary components for a complete stern tube seal system overhaul, including liner O-rings, gaskets, anodes and fasteners. Checking and replacing these seals is crucial to prevent leakage and emergency repairs while the vessel is at sea. Manufactured in Europe, RM Propulsion's Ocean Seal® is ideal for meeting your vessel's maintenance requirements.
